Complete Learning Journey

Each class level features a unique game-based approach to master specific mathematical concepts

6

Number Quest

Island Adventure

Players sail between islands solving factorization, LCM/GCD and prime puzzles to gather resources.

Learning Objectives:

  • Prime numbers and factorization
  • LCM and GCD calculations
  • Multiples and number patterns
  • Divisibility rules mastery
7

Geometry Builder

City Construction

Construct city buildings from geometric shapes; solve perimeter/area/angle tasks to unlock materials.

Learning Objectives:

  • Angles and their relationships
  • Perimeter and area calculations
  • Basic geometric theorems
  • Pythagorean theorem introduction
8

Algebra Arena

Mathematical Battles

Duel AI opponents by solving linear/quadratic equations and systems; correct moves drain opponent's HP.

Learning Objectives:

  • Linear equations mastery
  • Quadratic factoring
  • Systems of equations
  • Substitution and elimination
9

Trigonometry Tower

Angle Mastery

Scale a tower by solving angle problems, sine/cosine/tangent puzzles, and right-triangle problems.

Learning Objectives:

  • Trigonometric ratios
  • Solving triangles
  • Unit circle introduction
  • Practical applications
10

Probability Ludo

Strategic Thinking

A light card game where expected value and probability skills determine the best play.

Learning Objectives:

  • Basic probability theory
  • Expected value calculations
  • Combinations and permutations
  • Statistical decision making
11

Calculus Ramp

Motion Mastery

Craft ramps/trajectories for characters using derivatives and integrals to meet motion constraints.

Learning Objectives:

  • Limits and continuity
  • Derivatives and slopes
  • Basic integral calculations
  • Optimization fundamentals
12

Matrix Lab

Transformation Science

Solve transformations and systems using matrices to unlock lab equipment and progress in experiments.

Learning Objectives:

  • Matrix operations
  • Determinants and inverses
  • Linear transformations
  • Advanced system solving
